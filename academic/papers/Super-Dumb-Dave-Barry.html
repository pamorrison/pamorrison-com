BLAH BLAH I’M DAVE BARRY AND I’M SUPER DUMB

A reaction to “Guys vs. Men” by Dave Barry

 

An Undeserved Introduction

         I will begin this piece by saying that I don’t want to do it.  I disagree with the premise laid out by Dave Barry, and I think he plays into some really stupid gender stereotypes, and I generally find his characterization of “Guys” faulty and groundless and his characterization of women ridiculously pandering. But hey, it’s a pointless challenge, so Dave Barry argues that I’m going to love it only because of that. There’s no way he’s wrong about that, is there?

 

Dave Barry’s Stupid Ideas are Stupid

         “Humorist Dave Barry”. What a fantastically bland title for a fantastically bland mind. There exists a major difference between the idea of being “manly” and the idea of just being a “Guy” in the low-functioning brain of Dave Barry. I know this because he makes it SUPER DUPER OBVIOUS, EVERYBODY. According to Mr. Dave...er, Mr. Barry (why does he have two first names, it’s weird) Guys have defining characteristics that are common to almost all Guys. Firstly, Guys like cool gadgets, like computers that are far too powerful for their needs. He knows this because that’s what he has, and this means that all Guys must have computers that are far too powerful for their needs. Wowsers, good logic Barry. I mean Dave. Second, Guys like really pointless challenges, like racing against each other and apparently every sport. I know this is definitely true because I love all sports except no I don’t what the hell is he talking about. Thirdly, Guys don’t have a rigid moral code. They cheat often, because they’re sort of like dogs. They’re sort of like really, really stupid dogs. Apparently, every Guy would M E T A P H O R I C A L L Y poop on the floor if they knew they wouldn’t get caught. Did you know that? I didn’t know that. No one knows that, because it’s not true and particularly stupid, even for Super Dumb Dave Barry. By the way, I hope you caught Barry’s brilliant metaphor in which pooping on the floor = cheating, because it was brilliant, obvs.  Barrison David doesn’t think guys are scum.  He thinks they’re kind of LIKE scum.  Ok, they’re scum.  He says early on in this “reflection” essay (What is it he’s reflecting on, exactly?  A scummy pond? A dull, dirty mirror? A particularly well-polished urinal?) that men tend to attach great importance to Manhood, and that this gives Guys a bad name.  I’d say that his characterization of Guys coincides with the name “dumb [redacted]”. Well, I agree.  Dave Barry is the only “Guy” I know and is indeed a dumb [redacted]. And this [redacted] has produced exactly what you’d expect: a pile of excrement.  Hooray for “humorists.”

Paul A. Morrison
